DOYOUNG ALBUM RANKINGS

Here are my rankings of the songs within Doyoung's albums!
These are based on my personal opinions and which albums I would recommend to others, obviously opinions differ!

YOUTH

  1. Rest
  2. Serenade
  3. Beginning
  4. Dallas Love Field
  5. Lost In California
  6. From Little Wave
  7. Little Light
  8. Rewind
  9. Time Machine
  10. Warmth
BACK TO HOME

This album is a great introduction to Doyoung's personal style! I will say, Doyoung's pop-rock style is one that I love a lot and so when I first listened to this album, I knew it'd be one that I'd enjoy listening to repeatedly.

In particular, Rest is a standout track that showcases Doyoung's vocal range and emotional depth. His performances of this song just blow me away every time. He truly delivers an emotional and powerful performance.

Warmth is the song I ranked last on the album, but it still has its own unique charm and emotional appeal. I mostly put it there because I was stuck on picking the bottom few songs (I truly love every song on this album) and I feel like that song is less versatile in the sense that it might not fit as well in many scenarios if that makes sense?

Soar

  1. Wake From The Dark
  2. Memory
  3. Eternity
  4. First Step
  5. Sonnet
  6. Still
  7. Be My Light
  8. Sand Box
  9. Luminous
  10. Just Friends
BACK TO HOME

This album is a great continuation of Doyoung's personal style! It's really a masterpiece that gets better and better the more I tune in. The style of the songs fits Doyoung's personality and his voice really well.

Wake From The Dark is my current favourite song from the album, the lyrics are really powerful and the melody is so catchy. It has Doyoung's 'signature style': his crazy high notes and hightened emotional delivery, again highlighted in his concert performance!.
