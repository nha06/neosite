YUTA ALBUM RANKINGS

Here are my rankings of the songs within Yuta's albums!
These are based on my personal opinions, obviously opinions differ between people! I will also be giving my reasoning for certain songs.

Depth

  1. Butterfly (Depth Ver.)
  2. Off The Mask
  3. Save You
  4. Prisoner
  5. Goodbye
  6. Bad Euphoria
  7. LAST SONG
BACK TO HOME

This album is a great introduction to Yuta's personal style! I'm not the biggest rock/metal fan but these songs still managed to capture my attention. The flow of the album is great, the songs are all really good and the production quality is excellent.

Butterfly is on the top of the list since it's a song I believe can be listened to repeatedly without getting tired of it, at any point of the year. It was first performed as his solo performance in the NCT 127: THE LINK tour, but had been rearranged for the official release. Overall, it's a really pleasing and memorable song.

PERSONA

  1. Twisted Paradise
  2. When I'm Not Around
  3. EMBER
  4. If We Lose It All Tonight
  5. Get Out Of My Mind
  6. New World
  7. Two Of Us
  8. TO LOVE SOMEONE
  9. KNOCK KNOCK
  10. Possiboo-hoo
BACK TO HOME

This album is a great showcase of Yuta's versatility as an artist. The songs are well-produced and the themes are engaging. It's a great continuatiion from Depth and I can't wait to keep watching Yuta grow as a solo artist.

Twisted Paradise is my favourite song from the album, it's a really catchy song and I love the vibe of it. It's again a song that I can listen to on repeat without getting tired of it. I honestly love this song.

An honourable mention to my list is TO LOVE SOMEONE is also a really good song, it's a bit more mellow than the other songs in the album but it's still really good. I love the lyrics of this song and it has a really nice melody!
