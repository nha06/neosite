ABOUT NEOSITE

✦ THE PROJECT

NEOSITE is a fan-made project I built initially as a way to consolidate my knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript — but I wanted to do it in a way that was actually fun, hence the theme of NCT, my favourite group! I hope it can be a useful resource for other NCT fans, whether you're new to the fandom or have been here for a while.

✦ WHAT'S HERE

You can explore NCT album rankings — obviously biased since they're mine! But if you want to use them as a way to discover new songs or albums, that's great. There's also a timeline of NCT's units and subunits to help anyone getting into the group. I know it can be really overwhelming, especially with so many members, subunits, and things happening every single week.

✦ COMING SOON

I'm planning to build out a masterlist of content — possibly with a random content generator so you don't have to scroll through hundreds of videos on the NCT channel — as well as more diverse quizzes to test your knowledge or just waste some time with. There are lots of ideas in the pipeline, both features and design improvements!

✦ A NOTE

This is all coming together in my own time, by myself, so updates will happen as regularly as I can manage. Thanks for visiting NEOSITE and I hope you enjoy it!
