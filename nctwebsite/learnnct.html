NCT's Unit Timeline

scroll and click on the pictures for more detailed info!

2016

NCT debuts with NCT U and the song "일곱 번째 감각 (The 7th Sense)".

This debut included the members: Taeyong, Doyoung, Ten, Jaehyun and Mark, Taeyong being the leader of NCT as a whole.

DEBUT DATE: 09th April 2016

Full lineup details: Taeyong, Doyoung, Ten, Jaehyun, Mark

Leader: Taeyong

Debut stage: SM Town Live 2016

Fun fact: This was NCT’s first ever performance!

2016

NCT 127 debuts with the song "소방차 (Fire Truck)".

This lineup included the members: Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, WinWin, Mark and Haechan with Taeyong as the leader of this unit.

DEBUT DATE: 07th July 2016

Full lineup details: Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, WinWin, Mark, Haechan

Leader: Taeyong

Debut stage: SM Town Live 2016

Fun fact: This was NCT’s first ever performance!

2016

NCT Dream debuts with the song "Chewing Gum".

This lineup included the members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle and Jisung. There was no initial leader of the unit due to their graduation concept, but Mark, as the oldest member usually took on these responsibilities.

DEBUT DATE: 25th August 2016

Full lineup details: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle, Jisung

2017

Johnny and Doyoung are added as new members of NCT 127.

Their debut song was "무한적아 (Limitless)"

RELEASE DATE: 06th January 2017

Full lineup details: Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, WinWin, Mark, Haechan

2017

Due to a sustained back injury, Jaemin was unable to participate in activities.

NCT Dream continued as a unit without him that year, going on to release 마지막 첫사랑 (My First and Last) and We Young.

Full lineup details: Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, WinWin, Mark, Haechan

2018

NCT 2018 is a special project unit that includes all 18 members of NCT at the time.

They released the album "Empathy" with multiple title tracks, notably Black on Black, in which all members partook in the performance.

This marked the addition of two new members: Jungwoo and Lucas, as well as the return of Jaemin after his recovery.

RELEASE DATE: 14th March 2018

Full lineup details: Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, WinWin, Mark, Haechan
