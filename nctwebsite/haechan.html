BACK TO HOME

This album may just be my favourite of all time! It's a really great showcase of Haechan's talents and the songs are all really good. The production quality is excellent and the flow of the album is one of the best. The transitions between songs are smooth and obviously well thought out (e.g., from the Outro to Camera Lights, I would loop the album over and over just to get that same satisfaction.)

Should Be is a masterpiece. The vocals, production, and overall execution make it a standout track. I personally LOVE R&B and this song is a perfect example of how to do it right. I don't think I could ever get sick of this song.

The fact that Talented is last also says a lot about the quality of the album. I was genuinely struggling on picking which songs to put towards the end since I genuinely love the entire album. Just know that all the tracks sit between a 9.5-10/10 in my opinion. The Talented dance performance is also insane! I just love this album so much.
